Introduction

PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN ANAK-ANAK YATIM JOHOR (RUMAH BARKAT JOHOR)


VISION

Creating the Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-anak Yatim Johor(PKAYJ) to ultimate dream home


MISION

Give awareness to orphaned children that being an orphan is not a barrier to achieving great life goals


GOAL

Striving to produce knowledgeable, moral people to achieve personal well-being for harmony, prosperity and well-being in this world and the afterlife


Terms Requirements to Enter Rumah Barkat Johor

1. Orphans

2. Muslims

3. 6 years old and not above 17 years old

4. Income does not exceed RM1000 per month

5. Doctor's confirmation report
